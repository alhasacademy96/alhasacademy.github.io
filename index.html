Greetings, stranger.

Welcome to my personal homepage! I am a Software Engineer, having recently joined the University of Camrbdige's Department of Zoology for a research project as the sole Research Software Engineer.

Before my current role, I worked as a Software Engineer (later promoted to Technical Lead) at the Leverhulme Centre for the Future of Intelligence Institution (LCFI), University of Cambridge. My primary responsibilities were to develop, improve, and maintain the Animal-AI research environment. If interested, see the GitHub Repository here.

I received my MSc in Artificial Intelligence from King's College London in 2022, where I worked under the supervision of Dr. Joshua Murphy to develop a novel algorithm to predict whether a Twitter (now X) tweet was factual or fictitious. You can find my MSc Dissertation paper in my GitHub profile here.

In my spare time, I love reading books to expand my knowledge and explore new ideas. I am also an avid gamer (and game developer), always on the lookout for the next great virtual adventure.
